## 4. Description (long, for the project page)

You can use this as the main body text (or shorten for “Description” and use the rest in a “Features” list):

```
Money Survivor is a fast-paced top-down action roguelike (bullet-heaven style) set in a cyberpunk financial apocalypse. You play as a corporate CEO defending yourself with financially themed weapons against waves of bankers, auditors, and bosses.

• SURVIVE: Move through an office arena. Don’t leave the play area. Avoid obstacles. Enemies and bosses get stronger over time.
• FIGHT: Pick up to 3 weapons (Aimed Bullet, Coin Toss, Bill Whip, Compound Interest, Credit Card, Cryptominer, Stock Options). Each weapon can be upgraded to level 10.
• LEVEL UP: Collect XP orbs, fill the bar, then choose one of three cards: new weapon, weapon upgrade, or power-up. Bosses drop chests with random rewards.
• POWER-UPS: Health Insurance, Gold Rush, Hedge Fund, Black Market, Diversified Portfolio, Insider Trading, Tax Evasion, Overclock, Duplicate Projectile.
• BOSSES: IRS, CEO, and MegaBoss spawn on timers. They’re big, hit hard, and drop chests. Survive long enough and the pace becomes insane.

Your score is your Net Worth (enemies killed × 50). Only your best run is saved and shown on the main menu and game over screen.

Built in Unity. Supports keyboard and gamepad.
```
